Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping at MaxyKeys

What is MaxyKeys Music Shopping?
MaxyKeys Music Shopping is Ghana's premier online store for music software, instruments, and studio gadgets. We provide authentic products from top brands like FL Studio, Yamaha, Roland, Fender, and more.
Are your products genuine and licensed?
Yes! All our music software comes with official licenses, and all hardware instruments are 100% authentic from authorized distributors. We never sell counterfeit products.
Do you offer support after purchase?
Absolutely! We provide technical support for all software installations and hardware setup. Contact us via WhatsApp at 0503139412 for assistance.
Can I visit your physical store?
Abesim-Nkrankrom Road, Sunyani (Saint James Clinic). Visit us Monday-Saturday, 9am-7pm.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Paystack payments (credit/debit cards, mobile money), bank transfers, and cash on delivery within Accra. All online payments are secure and encrypted.
Is Paystack payment safe?
Yes! Paystack is PCI-DSS compliant and used by thousands of businesses in Ghana. Your card details are never stored on our servers.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
You can cancel within 1 hour of placing your order. Contact us immediately at 0503139412. Once shipped, cancellation may not be possible.
Do you offer installment payments?
Currently, we don't offer installment plans. However, we have regular discounts and promotions. Follow our social media for updates!
How long does delivery take?
Accra: 1-2 business days. Other regions (Kumasi, Takoradi, Tema): 2-4 business days. Remote areas: 5-7 business days.
How much is shipping?
Shipping fees vary by location and product size. Contact admin at 0503139412 for accurate delivery fees. Free pickup available at our Accra store.
Do you ship nationwide?
Yes! We ship to all 16 regions of Ghana including Greater Accra, Ashanti, Western, Eastern, Central, Volta, Northern, and Upper East/West regions.
Can I track my order?
Yes! Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via WhatsApp or email.
Do you offer warranties on products?
Yes! All hardware instruments come with 1-year manufacturer warranty. Software licenses are non-refundable but include lifetime support.
Can I return a product if I don't like it?
We accept returns within 7 days for unopened, unused products. Software licenses cannot be returned once activated. See our Returns Policy page for details.
What if my product arrives damaged?
Contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a replacement or refund within 48 hours.
Do you offer music production training?
Yes! We offer basic to advanced training in FL Studio, Ableton Live, and music production. Contact us for schedule and fees.
